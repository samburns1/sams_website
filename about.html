if you somehow missed it, i love planes, and if you are around me enough, you'll notice i'm usually staring up. the la aerospace keeps me busy. my current favorite plane is the a350.


aside from aerospace, i am super interested in other facets of engineering. im an ai optimist, and its use cases and applications excite me. ai allows me to expand out of my areas of expertice, creating an opportunity to rapidly absorb new information. i am a quick learner, which pairs well with my passion to learn and create. llms let me do more, and i firmly believe that understanding how to use them as a learning supplement is a critical skillset, especially in times where tech is advancing at such a rapid rate. we need to keep up somehow!
